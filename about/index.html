About Us

About Nationwide Recycling Inc

Our Story

Founded in 2005, Nationwide Recycling Inc has been serving the Edison area with honest, reliable scrap metal recycling services. What started as a small operation has grown into a trusted name in the recycling industry, built on a simple principle: Fair Weight, Fair Price.

Our team treats every customer — from individual homeowners to large commercial contractors — with the same respect and fairness. We invest in certified scales, experienced staff, and responsible recycling practices to ensure you get the best value for your materials.

What We Accept

All ferrous and non-ferrous metals including copper, aluminum, steel, iron, brass, stainless steel, lead, electronic waste, and more.

Payment

We offer same-day payment via cash, check, wire transfer. No waiting — get paid when you drop off your material.

Our Commitment

Every load is weighed on our certified scale and priced at current market rates. We believe in transparency — you can watch the scale and see exactly what you're being paid for.
