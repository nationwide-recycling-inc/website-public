Our Services

Scrap Metal Buying

At Nationwide Recycling Inc, we purchase all types of ferrous and non-ferrous scrap metals at competitive, market-based prices. Whether you have a small load of copper wire or a truckload of structural steel, we make the process fast and easy. Drive up to our certified scale, get weighed, unload your material, and get paid on the spot. We accept copper, aluminum, brass, stainless steel, iron, steel, lead, and more. Our experienced staff can help identify and sort your materials to ensure you get the best possible price.

  • All metals accepted
